About

Mohamed Lemine Babahmoudy, a mechanical engineer (B.Eng, 2020), brings over five years of expertise in computational design and analysis, delivering innovative solutions for renewable energy, water systems, and mining industries. Proficient in CAD (SolidWorks, AutoCAD, FreeCAD), FEA/CFD (Abaqus, ANSYS, OpenFOAM), and programming (Python, Fortran, MATLAB), he has completed 20+ Upwork projects with consistent 5-star ratings. Trilingual in English, French, and Arabic, his global experience spans Mauritania, Tunisia, and France, collaborating with NGOs and industrial clients. Notable achievements include optimizing a solar water pump for 15% efficiency gains, enhancing sustainable development.

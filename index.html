About Me

With a Mechanical Engineering degree from ENIT, Tunisia (September 2020), I bring over 4 years of experience in finite element analysis (FEA), computational fluid dynamics (CFD), and numerical computation. Having delivered 20+ projects across industrial, energy, and biomedical fields for clients in Saudi Arabia, Canada, UK, France, Belgium, and Germany, I offer global expertise. My work spans design sketching to manufacturing, validated through advanced simulation techniques and multiphysics analysis, with a passion for material mechanics, chemistry, thermodynamics, vibration, and all related aspects.

I have contributed to R&D at Mecamix (https://mecamix-rd.com/home.html) and Lamcube (https://lamcube.univ-lille.fr/), and previously served as a CAD trainer, where I honed my skills in sharing knowledge. I am dedicated to supporting engineers in mechanical, structural, and civil engineering fields, leveraging tools like AutoCAD, SolidWorks, ANSYS, and Abaqus—both commercial and open-source—while fostering collaboration and learning.

My trilingual proficiency (Arabic, French, English) ensures clear communication, and my commitment to ethical, high-quality work drives innovative solutions. Whether you need precise designs, advanced simulations, or personalized tutoring, I’m here to help you succeed.
